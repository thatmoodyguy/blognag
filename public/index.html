BlogNag (beta)

Use Twitter to keep your blog fresh!

By watching your blog on a daily basis, BlogNag knows when you haven't posted in a while, and send you a tweet to remind you to get your butt in gear!

There's no signup, no charge, and no hassle. BlogNag is entirely controlled through your favorite Twitter client!


Tweet: @blognag lifeasacoder.com
Adds the blog at lifeasacoder.com to your nag list. You'll get reminded if you go without posting for 7 days.

Tweet: @blognag lifeasacoder.com 3
Adds the blog at lifeasacoder.com to your nag list. You'll get reminded if you go without posting for 3 days.

Tweet: @blognag lifeasacoder.com 3
Adds the blog at lifeasacoder.com to your nag list. You'll get reminded if you go without posting for 3 days.

Tweet: @blognag remove lifeasacoder.com
Removes the blog at lifeasacoder.com from your nag list.

Tweet: @blognag remove all
Removes all blogs from your nag list.

Tweet: @blognag list
Sends you a list of all of the blogs on your nag list.

Tweet: @blognag help
Sends you a link to this page!
